What is a Linear & Circular Welding SPM?

A Linear & Circular Special Purpose Machine (SPM) is a dedicated automation system designed to perform high-precision welding operations on either straight seams or circular components. Unlike general-purpose robotic arms, our SPMs are custom-engineered for specific product geometries, ensuring maximum stability, repeatability, and throughput.

Whether it's the longitudinal seam of a pressure vessel or the circumferential weld of an automotive flange, our machines integrate advanced servo motion control with sophisticated sensor feedback to deliver X-ray quality welds every single time.
