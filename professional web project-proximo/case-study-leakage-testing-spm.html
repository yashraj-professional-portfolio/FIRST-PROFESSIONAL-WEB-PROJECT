What is a Leakage Testing SPM?

A Leakage Testing Special Purpose Machine (SPM) is a custom-engineered automation system designed to detect microscopic leaks and pressure drops in industrial components. Whether it's automotive engine parts, pharmaceutical vials, or aerospace valves, our SPM ensures that every unit meets stringent quality standards.
