Technical Overview

Redefining Industrial Holemaking

The Proximo High-Precision Drilling SPM is a vertically integrated solution designed for high-volume, tight-tolerance components. Utilizing a multi-stage servo-driven architecture, it eliminates the variabilities of manual operation.
